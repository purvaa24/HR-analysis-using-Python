HR Analytics Project | 2 Million Employee Records Analyzed

This dataset contains HR information for employees of a multinational corporation (MNC). It includes 2 Million (20 Lakhs) employee records with details about personal identifiers, job-related attributes, performance, employment status, and salary information. The dataset can be used for HR analytics, including workforce distribution, attrition analysis, salary trends, and performance evaluation.

Key Insights:
1. Workforce Distribution
Identified department-wise and job-level employee distribution.
Highlighted areas with high workforce concentration for better resource planning.

2. Attrition Analysis
Higher attrition among early-career employees.
Employees with lower performance ratings showed increased exit probability.

3. Salary Trends
Clear salary progression across experience and job levels.
Identified pay variations across departments and performance categories.

4. Performance Insights
Strong relationship between performance ratings and promotion frequency.
High performers demonstrated better retention rates.

5. Business Impact:
Supports strategic workforce planning
Enables data-driven salary benchmarking
Helps HR teams detect attrition risks early
Improves performance evaluation systems

Tools Used:
Python (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib)

This project enhanced my ability to handle large datasets, perform data cleaning & analysis, and create meaningful visualizations for business decision-making.
